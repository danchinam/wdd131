Abuja, Nigeria

Nigeria, located in West Africa, was formed through the merging of British colonies and protectorates. Its colonial history began in the late 19th century when the British established control over the region. The British colonized the area through both direct and indirect rule, exploiting Nigeria's rich natural resources, including palm oil, cocoa, and later, oil. During this time, the country's ethnic groups and cultures were divided into regions like the Northern, Western, and Eastern Protectorates. After years of unrest, World War II, and growing nationalist movements, Nigeria gained its independence from Britain on October 1, 1960, becoming a sovereign nation.
